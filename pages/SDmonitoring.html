Introduction

I spent 5 years at the University of Utah conducting research with the Telescope Array Cosmic Ray Observatory with Charles Jui as my thesis advisor. I am currently writing my thesis for my PhD in Physics on the Ultra-High Energy Cosmic Ray energy spectrum (UHECR). My thesis focuses on collecting, processing, and reconstructing data of the new Telescope Array expansion (TAx4) using a hybrid analysis. Hybrid analysis utilizes information from both Surface Detector (SD) and Flourescence Detector (FD) to constrain the reconstructed shower profile. My analysis has yielded the first TAx4 hybrid cosmic ray energy spectrum. I also report the performance of TAx4 and present data-MC comparisions.
